## When to use

[Dropdown](https://primer.style/css/components/dropdown) | [Select menu](https://primer.style/css/components/select-menu) | [Popover](https://primer.style/css/components/popover) | [Box overlay](https://primer.style/css/components/box-overlay)
--- | --- | --- | ---
Use when there are only a **few options** to choose from. | Use when there are **many options**. Or features like multiple selections and filtering is needed. | Use to **inform users** about a new product feature. | Use to get a user's full attiontion because they have to **make a choice**.
